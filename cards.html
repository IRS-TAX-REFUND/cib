Central Illinois Bank Debit Card

It’s the better way to make all of your everyday purchases. The Central Illinois Bank Debit Card can be used wherever MasterCard is accepted. Your debit card works quicker than checks, is safer than cash, and automatically deducts purchases from your checking account.

You can even earn points on purchases with your Central Illinois Bank Debit Card. Register your Central Illinois Bank Debit Card in our rewards program today. The rewards program is available at no charge to all debit card users. Visit www.cibmrewards.rewardschoices.com to learn where you earn points.

Your Central Illinois Bank Debit Card is also a part of a large surcharge free ATM network. Locate the ATM that is most convenient for you.
