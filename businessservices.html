Capital Markets

Today's business professionals know the importance of careful cash management and short-term investing of excess cash balances. Accessing the broader capital markets on a frequent basis can be costly. Central Illinois Bank enables organizations to efficiently make frequent short-term investments through select deposits and other non-deposit products and services. We can help you design a program to put excess cash to work each night or over longer periods of time in a convenient and effective way.

  • Certificates of Deposit (91 days to 7 years)
  • Money Market Accounts (interest earning deposit with unlimited sweeps to and 6 transfers and withdrawals from per month)
  • Repurchase Agreements (overnight sweep accounts to one year terms)*
  • U.S. Treasury Bills and Government Agency Discount Notes (one year and less)*

Central Illinois Bank offers both deposit and non-investment products. Certificates of Deposit of $250,000 are FDIC insured. *Repurchase Agreements, U.S. Treasury Bills and Government Agency Discount Notes are not a bank deposit, obligation or guaranteed by the bank and are NOT FDIC insured. These investment products are subject to investment risk, including potential principal loss.
